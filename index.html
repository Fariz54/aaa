Bachelor of Engineering

BE full form is Bachelor of Engineering. The course duration is four years and the emphasises is on theoretical understanding as well as the engineering component of science. The BE syllabus and subjects covers engineering geology, engineering mathematics, basic material and surveying, etc. The BE job scope is vast providing employment opportunities in research, automobile industry, information technology, etc.

Master of Engineering

A Master of Engineering (MEng) is a postgraduate degree in engineering that usually takes one to two years to complete. It provides advanced training in a specific area of engineering and prepares students for professional careers in the field. The curriculum of an MEng program typically includes both coursework and a research project, and the program may be offered as a thesis or non-thesis option.

Masters of Business Administration

A MBA is a postgraduate degree focused on business administration. The core courses in an MBA program cover various areas of business administration such as accounting, human resources, business communication, business ethics, business law, business strategy, finance, management, entrepreneurship, marketing, and operations management in a manner most relevant to management analysis and strategy.
